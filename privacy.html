Conference Privacy Policy

Kent Beck (thinkies.org) is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This Policy explains how we gather and use the information that we collect during and after registration for Thinkies World Congress. This policy may change over time. Amendments to this statement will be posted at http://thinkies.org and will be effective when posted. Your continued use of this site following the posting of any amendment, modification or change shall constitute your acceptance thereof.

Consent

By registering for conference updates or signing up for the conference, you expressly consent to the collection and use by thinkies.org of personally identifiable information according to this Privacy Policy laid out below.

Registration

During the registration process for Thinkies World Congress, thinkies.org will collect the following personally identifiable information about you: your name, email address, and Country of Residence. This information will be used for Thinkies World Congress registration.

Communications

thinkies.org occasionally will contact registrants signed up for Thinkies World Congress with email updates for the current or future conference. Registrants can opt-out of such communications at any time by contacting us or by using the unsubscribe link. We also may provide information to regulatory authorities and law enforcement officials in accordance with applicable law or when we otherwise believe in good faith that the law requires it. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information or e-mail addresses with external organizations or individuals.

Links to Other Web Sites

thinkies.org may work with other companies' websites to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include Thinkies World Congress registration, sending e-mail, and processing credit card payments. They have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, and this is subject to their privacy policies. thinkies.org is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. If you want to know the privacy policies of the other websites, please refer to the organization sponsoring such website.

Conditions of Use, Notices, and Revisions

If you choose to attend Thinkies World Congress, your visit and any dispute over privacy is subject to this notice, including limitations on damages, arbitration of disputes, and application of the law of California in the United States. If you have any concern about privacy at Thinkies World Congress, please contact us with a thorough description, and we will try to resolve it. Circumstances change constantly, and our Privacy Notice will change also. We may e-mail periodic reminders of our notices and conditions, unless you have instructed us not to, but you should check our website frequently to see recent changes. Unless stated otherwise, our current Privacy Notice applies to all information that we have about you. We stand behind the promises we make, however, and will never materially change our policies and practices to make them less protective of customer information collected in the past without the consent of affected customers.
